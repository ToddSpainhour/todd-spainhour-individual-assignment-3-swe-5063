About Us

Hi! I'm Todd and I own Victory Board Games. For over 20 years, we’ve been an independently-owned friendly local game store based out of Nashville. You may remember us back from our days in Murfreesboro. We’re the same friendly community of gamers as ever, specializing in board games, tabletop games, and more. We also host store tournaments every day of the week. Drop by to check out the store, try a new game, and make some friends!
